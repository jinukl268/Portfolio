00:01 Editor · Videographer · Photographer

I'm Jinu Lorance — I shoot it, frame it, and cut it. My work spans three roles that usually get split across three people: capturing footage on location, taking stills, and editing the final piece for YouTube, social media ads, and promotional videos.

Because I handle the whole chain, the edit isn't an afterthought — I shoot with the cut already in mind, and I edit knowing exactly what the camera saw. My process starts with the story, not the timeline: what's the hook, where does interest dip, what earns the next 10 seconds.
